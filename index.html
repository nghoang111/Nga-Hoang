Project Ideas

Overview

This project aims to develop a web application which enables users to interact with their favorite skincare blogger/vlogger. It will allow users to log in, sign up, real time chat, post messages and interact with other posts. It helps the skincare blogger/vlogger to increase interactions with their followers, build sustainable relationship while sharing valuable knowledge and tips from their experiences to others.

Motivation

Nowadays, people are more attentive to their outlook and appearance, especially their face and skin. According to Park and Nam (2012), the awareness about skin health is not just for women but men also care about their skin as there are more male visit skin treatment facilities. Skincare is not only about beauty, but it is also about health. Based on a study in England, skin diseases like skin cancer and melanoma are increasing (Vallejo-Torres et al. 2014). Therefore, with decent care and protection, it helps prevent those skin diseases. The skincare industry has grown enormously, multiple brands and products, the competition is great and so is the profitability. According to Euromonitor (2019), the industry records current value growth at 1% in 2018 to reach approximately KRW7,100 billion and is still expected to growth by 2023 in Korea. One of the key players in the industry is social influencers like beauty bloggers and vloggers. They share their experiences and knowledge on the internet and social media platforms. As they reached the crowd and increase popularity, big players in the industry will offer them a partner contract and through their popularity, business can sell more goods and increase brand awareness while the consumers benefit from the information shared. Thus, an application where beauty influencers can interact to their followers is important. It supports the bloggers and vloggers to reach to and take care of their existing followers and answer questions send directly from them. They can maintain the interactions, share more specified information to a direct group and thus, enhance their popularity.

Description

IThe web application will allow users to register as a new user and to log in their account. They can then customize their profile page and input more personal information. The features of this application are post message, reply to message, like and dislike message, comment on a post, delete a post and real time chat with the host influencer. They can then post a message on the message board and other users can then reply to the post. They can also like or dislike other messages on the message board. When the host influencer wished to open the public real time chat, users can post questions and communicate with the host instantly.

Tools and techniques

The messaging chat application will be developed using Google Firebase and its resources, for example, Firebase Realtime database, Firebase Firestore, Firebase functions and Firebase hosting. The software involving are Visual Studio Code, WebStorm, Slack and Trello. The hardware involving are personal computers and mobile devices.

Skills required

The skills involved are user interface design, cross-browser web application development, backend computing and programming language skills. User interface design skills will help with building a high-quality user interface, which will help the users interact effectively with the app. People would more likely enjoy a user-friendly with superior design and run smoothly app than a poor user interface one (Toth 2017). Moreover, the ability to build the web application that works across browsers, such as Chrome and Safari, is also important. It helps expand the possibilities and opportunities for the app. Backend computing, which involves security, database management and hardware interaction, is also important. Everything involving IT involves security issues, thus, security knowledge is essential while developing the app. Hence, skills involving Firestore NoSQL and Google Cloud Function would be a good example. Finally, programming language is also important. According to Toth (2017), the developer needs to be familiar with programming languages like C# and Java and to be trained in web development languages like HTML 5 and CSS. For example, React JS skill for frontend JavaScript and Node JS for backend JavaScript. Therefore, a team of diverse expertise members, such as security, programming, UI design, etc., would be fantastic to develop this app. Teamwork, communication and constructive thinking are the crucial soft skills that help them run the team together.

Outcome

This application will allow social media influencers to have a customized channel to interact with their followers, to take care of them and to maintain the interactions. They can answer questions related to sensitive topics that they do not want to disclose publicly just to receive unwanted criticisms. For example, topics like which brands or products they do not like or do not work, which service provider do they go to get something done, etc. Something they would share out of their pure experience, but somebody would see it as an advertisement or a bullying act. With this application, they now have a more secure and private channel to stay connected and share more valuable information with those who really in need.
